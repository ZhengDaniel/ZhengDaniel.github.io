Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp Bread Flour
  • 5 Tbsp Water
  • 5 Tbsp Whole Milk
    1. In a saucepan, whisk bread flour and water until no lumps remain.
    2. Add whole milk and whisk.
    3. Put saucepan over medium heat and continuously stir until it becomes a thick pudding like consistency. ~1-3 min
    4. Let cool completely.

What is Tangzhong?

Tangzhong is an asian method of making bread. By making a thick pudding like mixture, we are able to add more moisture into the bread by incorporating liquid in solid form. You can add this to yeast dough to give a softer and moister bread.

    Tangzhong Results

    1. Softer bread
    2. Bread stays fresh longer
    3. More moist
    4. Easier to knead
    5. Lighter and fluffier
