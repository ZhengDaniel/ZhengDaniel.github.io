Mochi (Microwave Method)

By Olivia K

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Mochiko Sweet Rice Flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  1. Whisk together Mochiko Sweet Rice Flour and sugar.
  2. Add water and mix well.
  3. Cover bowl loosely with plastic wrap and microwave for 1 min on high.
  4. Stir with a rubber spatula or wooden spoon.
  5. Cover again and microwave again for 1 min.
  6. Repeat step 4.
  7. Microwave for 30 min. It should be translucent by now.
  8. Turn onto a surface floured with corn starch.
  9. Shape however you like. You can alio fill it with fillings such as red bean paste.
