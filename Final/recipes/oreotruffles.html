Oreo Truffles

By Olivia K

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of oreos
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup semi sweet chocolate (melted)
  • 1/3 cup white chocolate (melted)(optional)
  • 2 tbsp oreo crumbs(optional)

Directions

  1. Mash oreos in a plastic bag or in a food processor.
  2. Add in cream cheese and mix well. It should be a wet paste.
  3. Using your hands, form paste into balls.
  4. Freeze the balls for at least 30 min.
  5. Using a fork, dip each ball into melted semi sweet chocolate. Be sure to fully cover with chocolate.
  6. Place on a lined tray and freeze for at least an hour.
  7. Drizzle melted white chocolate and sprinkle oreo crumbs if desired.
